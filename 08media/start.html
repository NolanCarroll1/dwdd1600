Where to Start?

Sometimes it can be confusing to know where to start with Magic. With so many packs, cards and pre-constructed decks to choose from, you can become paralyzed with all the choices. Here are some quick resources to help you make a choie when buying something for the first time. Remember, people at your local game store will also always be willing to help you out as well!
