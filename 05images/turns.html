The Anatomy of a Turn

A game of Magic begins with two players drawing seven cards from the top of thier randomly shuffled library, determining who will take the first turn via a die roll and then the first player going through the various steps and phases of their turn.

Steps and Phases

  1. Untap, Upkeep and Draw step
  2. First Main phase
  3. Combat
  4. Second Main phase
  5. Clean-Up step
